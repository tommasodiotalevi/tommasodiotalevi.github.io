List of outreach activities

- During my years as a master graduate, I was involved in an organisation named "Associazione Italiana Studenti di Fisica (AISF)" promoting collaborations among Italian physics students in an international and global context. 

- In July 2019, I was involved in a INFN CC3M "Comitato di Coordinamento III Missione" project for High School students, mainly focused as a training in High Energy Physics topics using specific tools and data provided by the ATLAS collaboration.

- From 2018 I continuously participate at the European Night of Researchers in Bologna, with a stand focusing on "Artificial Intelligence for High Energy Physics".

- In February 2024 I attended the event "Using Leonardo Supercomputer: computing HPC contest", organized by Emilia-Romagna region and CINECA.

- In January 2025, I organized a visit to the Bologna technopole, in the context of the workshop "Quasi Interactive analysis of big data with high throughput". In particular, the Leonardo supercomputer and the INFN-CNAF Tier-1 data center.

- In July 2025, I attended the event INFN STEAM Summer Camp, with a laboratory focused on "Artificial Intelligence and High Energy Physics". The event was targeted to high school students.
