List of outreach activities

- During my years as a master graduate, I was involved in an organisation named "Associazione Italiana Studenti di Fisica (AISF)" promoting collaborations among Italian physics students in an international and global context. 

- In 2018 and 2019 I participated at the European Night of Researchers in Bologna, with a stand focusing on "Artificial Intellgence for High Energy Physics".

- In July 2019, I was involved in a INFN CC3M "Comitato di Coordinamento III Missione" project for High School students, mainly focused as a training in High Energy Physics topics using specific tools and data provided by the ATLAS collaboration.
