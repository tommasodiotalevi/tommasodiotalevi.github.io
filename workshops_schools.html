Workshops

Workshop on "Quasi interactive analysis of big data with high throughput" (2025)

This workshop was held in Bologna (Italy) from the 8th to the 10th of January 2025. This workshop, part of the activities related to the Spoke2 (“Fundamental Research and Space Economy”) of the ICSC "National Center on HPC, Big Data and Quantum Computing", aims to describe an interactive cloud-based platform for high-rate analysis, based on declarative solutions and distributed resources. In particular, how to best utilize them from a user perspective, making scientific analysis applications even more efficient and faster. Link to agenda.

Workshop of the CMS experiment at the LHC (2024)

This workshop was held in Rome (Italy) from the 7th to the 9th of October 2024. A summary of the year by the italian community of the CMS experiment.

Workshop of the CMS experiment at the LHC (2021)

This workshop was held in Naples (Italy) from the 11th to the 13th of October 2021. A summary of the year by the italian community of the CMS experiment.

Schools

Course on porting code and algorithms to GPUs

This school was held from the 30th June to the 4th of July 2025 in Bologna (Italy). I co-organized the event, in the context of the the "National Center for HPC, Big Data, and Quantum Computing". The programme includes GPU programming and the usage of portability tools like Alpaka. Link to the school page here.

ESC 2022

This school was held from the 3rd to the 8th of October 2022, in Bertinoro (Italy). The programme includes introductory lectures on current trends in the evolution of processor architectures and parallel programming. This will be followed by more in-depth lectures on advanced programming with C++, efficient use of memory, floating-point computation and programming in a heterogeneous environment, exploiting the potential of multi-threaded computing, GPUs and cluster computing with MPI. Link to the school page here.

CMSvDAS 2020

This school was held online from the 23rd to the 30th of September 2020. The school was targeted to physicists interested in data analysis willing to extend their knowledge and skills on the fundamental tools and techniques for High Energy Physics analysis in CMS. Link to the school page here.

INFN School of statistics 2019

This school was held in Paestum (Salerno, Italy) from the 2nd to the 7th of June 2019. The school was targeted to physicists interested in data analysis willing to extend their knowledge and skills in the field of statistical methods for particle, astro-particle and nuclear physics. Link to the school page here.

  Third International School on Open Science Cloud 2019

This school was a multi-disciplinary mainly meant for PhD students, organized into two different tracks:
- Machine Learning methods and applications
- Comptuting Infrastructures (Cloud solutions and big data workflows)
During the school, several hands-on sessions were given to students and also a kaggle competition was held. Link to the school page here.
At the end of the competition, I was awarded with the second prize.
